About us

About CGIP

CG Industrial Park(CGIP) is Nepalâ€™s first ultramodern industrial village situated at Nawalparasi district, 185 km by road from Kathmandu and 23 km west of Narayanghat .CGIP spans over 137 acres of strategically located land adjacent to Mahendra Highway. In 1965, CG established its first Modern Hosiery Industry in Birgunj. Next development was by establishing a Biscuit Factory and Maida Mill in Duhabi, one of the very first industries in the area. Today it is the largest industrial area in Nepal.While establishing CGFP(CG Food Park) at Sainbu, it was realized that every time the same infrastructure was needed to establish an industry. This was when the Group decided to built an integrated world class industrial village having full-fledged infrastructural facilities to smoothen the progress of the establishment of industries in a short period decreasing the cost, time and efforts required to establish an industry.

Land Acquisition

The land was acquired in 1993 and the commercial production started in 1995. It was formally named as Ganga Devi Chaudhary Udyog Gram on 9th March 2003.

Key Facts

  • More than 137 acres of land
  • 6 different companies
  • 15 different units running simultaneously
  • 1,530 Employees
  • 4 Noodles plants producing around 450 million packets per year
  • 13 broken Noodles plants
